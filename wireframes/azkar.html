أَصْـبَحْنا وَأَصْـبَحَ المُلكُ لِلّٰه Asbahna wa asbahal-mulku lillah We have entered the morning and the kingdom belongs to Allah Abu Dawud 3 times Protection from harm
اللّٰهُ لاَ إِلَٰهَ إِلاَّ هُوَ Allahu la ilaha illa huwa Allah, there is no god but Him Bukhari 10 times Forgiveness of sins
لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلاَّ اللّٰهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ La ilaha illallahu wahdahu la sharika lahu There is no god but Allah, the One, there is no partner for Him Muslim 5 times Success in this life and the next
